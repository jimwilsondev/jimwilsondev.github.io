CRM version:1.0

This is a simple CRM app that helps you manage customer information, customer visit plan reminders, and group text messages to customers. This app is mainly for small businesses, self-employed customers with less resources, better maintenance of customer relationships, and saving human resources.


1. Customer Information:

You can add your customer information, such as avatar, name, phone number, email address, address, note information, especially phone and email, so you can remember your customer information at a glance.


2. Visit plan:

You can choose your customer to add a visit plan. After adding a visit plan, the app will send a reminder to visit the customer according to the reminder time you set.


3. Message template:

After you create a text message template, you can choose your customer to send SMS groupings and mass mailings, so that you can easily contact customers. You can add a holiday blessing template and a birthday greeting template to keep you in good relationship with your customers.


Note: When using email, you need to configure the email account in your phone, otherwise you will not be able to send it.
