PlanAssistant version:1.0

This is a simple plan list app, really simple! Life is short, 6 plans a day, easy to complete.

We recorded the first 7 days, the next 7 days and today's plan, and we can set up 6 plans every day, which is easy to use.

Different colors represent different states:

1. Yellow means no planning;

2. Red represents the plan is not completed;

3. Blue represents the plan has been completed.

Click the plan button at the bottom to switch the plan, double click to reschedule the plan, and long press to complete the plan.

Is it very simple and very easy to use!
